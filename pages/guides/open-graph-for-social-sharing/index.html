Some links show relevant titles and images while others don't explain the link at all. It comes down to the use of [Open Graph](http://ogp.me/) meta tags which are hidden from view inside the source of the page that's being sharing. Both Facebook and Twitter look for and make use of these tags whenever somebody shares your pages. You'll want to get these settings right to make the most of the effort and help out your fans who are sharing your site.
